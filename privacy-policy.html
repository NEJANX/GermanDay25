Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: June 7, 2025

Introduction

Zeit f端r Deutschland '25 ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you participate in our German culture competitions and use our Google Drive integration services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you submit entries to our competitions, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • School or institution name
  • Competition category selection
  • Artwork titles and descriptions

File Upload Information

When you upload artwork files, we may access:

  • File content for uploading to GoFile hosting service
  • File metadata (name, size, format)
  • Upload timestamps and status

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Processing and managing competition submissions
  • Facilitating file uploads to GoFile hosting service
  • Communicating with participants about competition updates
  • Organizing and judging competition entries
  • Displaying winners and showcasing artwork (with permission)

File Storage Integration

info Important: File Storage

Your submitted files are uploaded to GoFile, a third-party file hosting service. We only store links to access these files for judging purposes.

Our file upload system:

  • Uploads your artwork files to GoFile hosting service
  • Makes files publicly accessible via direct links for judging
  • Stores only the public file links and metadata in our database
  • Does not store actual files on our servers
  • Files are hosted by GoFile according to their terms of service

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • Competition Judging: Anonymous submissions shared with judges
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or legal process
  • Consent: With your explicit permission for promotional purposes

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
  • Firebase security rules for database access
  • Google OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we have collected
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • File Management: Contact us to manage or remove uploaded files
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your submitted data

Third-Party Services

Our application integrates with:

  • GoFile: For file hosting and storage
  • Firebase: For database and hosting services

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review:

  • GoFile Terms & Privacy
  • Firebase Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

Our service is designed for educational institutions and may be used by students under 18. We take extra care to protect children's privacy and comply with applicable laws. Parental or school consent should be obtained before students participate in our competitions.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
